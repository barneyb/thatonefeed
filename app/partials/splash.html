That One Feed

That One Feed is the minimalist's way of consuming your Feedly-managed content.

That One Feed lets you view your Feedly categories a single item at a time. It marks your items read as soon as you see them. It lets you consume content in an optimized way, with no fuss. It is sophisticated, yet unapologetically simple.

That One Feed doesn't do scrolling. It doesn't do individual feeds. It doesn't do tags. It doesn't require an account.

That One Feed is not for everyone. But if you want a simple viewer that gets out of your way, it is for you.
